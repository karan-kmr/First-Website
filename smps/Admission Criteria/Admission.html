PRE-PRIMARY SCHOOL ADMISSION 2018-2019

The entry class to the school is Pre Primary. There are 80 Seats. Process of Admission to Pre-Primary, commences from the 27.12.2017 for children who are 4+ years in age as on 31 March, 2018. DOCUMENTS TO BE ATTACHED WITH APPLICATION FORM TO BE FILLED IN BY SELECTED STUDENTS (ORIGINALS VERIFICATION)

  • Recent passport size colour photograph of the child and of the parents, duly pasted on specified place marked.
  • Attested photocopy of Municipal Birth Certificate.
  • Attested photocopy of Ration Card / Election ID Card / Passport (Proof of Residence). Photocopy of Aadhar Card.
  • Proof of Old Student of VIS Photocopy of Class XII School Leaving Certificate of VIS, Sector-10, Dwarka New Delhi-110075..

  • Pre-Primary is the entry class to Vandana International Sr. Sec. School, Sector-10 Dwarka, New Delhi-75. There are 80 seats out of which 20 seats are reserved for EWS/DG category. Process of admission to Pre-Primary will commence from 27.12.2017 for children who are 4+ years of age as on 31 March, 2018.

    Process of admission to Pre-Primary will commence from Dec 27,2017 for children who are 4 years of age as on April 1, 2018.

    Admission Procedure:-

    For admission to Pre-Primary the child should have completed 4 years as on 31-03-2018.

    • The registration form duly filled and received by the school office does not guarantee admission.
    • The list of selected candidates is put up on the school notice board for the admission procedure to be completed within the stipulated time.
    • Admission after the date specified by the office will not be considered under any condition.
